# Disclaimer — Research Use Only

**chromadose is NOT a medical device.**

chromadose is open-source software intended for academic research, method
development, and education in radiochromic film dosimetry. It is **not**
intended for diagnosis, treatment planning, treatment verification, or any
other clinical decision-making.

chromadose has not been evaluated, cleared, certified, or approved by:

- The U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
- The UK Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA)
- Any Notified Body under EU Regulation 2017/745 (MDR)
- Any other medical device regulatory authority

It carries no CE mark, no FDA clearance (510(k) or otherwise), and is not
developed under an ISO 13485 quality management system or IEC 62304 software
lifecycle process.

## Intended use

chromadose is intended for:

- Academic research into radiochromic film dosimetry methods
- Comparison and characterisation of multichannel reconstruction algorithms
- Educational use in medical physics training
- Method development and software engineering research

chromadose is **not** intended for:

- Clinical patient dose verification
- Treatment plan QA used to release plans for patient treatment
- Any use that informs a clinical decision affecting patient care
- Embedding in a clinical workflow without independent validation by a
  qualified medical physicist under an institutional QA programme
